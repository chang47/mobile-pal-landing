Turn Your Daily Fitness Into a Game

Mobile Pals Run is a fitness app that uses your steps to battle wild Mobile Pals and helps you become more fit!

Run in the Real World.

Become the Best in Another.

Mobile Pals are cute and active creatures that roam across the world. They like to work out with other people to become stronger to compete against other Mobile Pals. You've finally decidedt miok follow your dream to become the Mobile Pal World Champion!

Befriend new Mobile Pals, train them to become stronger, and then challenge other runners to become the best!


How to play


1) Select Your Workout

Pick a dungeon you want to go and train with your Pals in.


2) Put Your Phone Away

Put the app in your background. The app doesn't consume much battery!


3) Go Through Your Day

Mobile Pals Run is powered by your steps. Try to take as many steps as you can in your day!


4) Celebrate Your Progress

After a hard day of work, come back and see how much stronger your Pals have become!


Get Motivated

Make Being Active Fun

Compete head on against Arena Leaders around the world of Mobile Pals. Each Arena will require you to strategize your team and push your physical limits through their workouts if you hope to emerge victorious!


Get Rewards

More Steps, More Rewards

Mobile Pals Run rewards you for being active in the game. By actively playing the game and logging your steps, you'll be awarded Pal eggs that gives you new rare and powerful friends.
